Official Lexicon of the Suquelish Republic

This repository contains the official digital lexicon and language reference for the Suquelish (Suquelinese) language. It serves as the primary source for vocabulary, structure, and language documentation maintained by the Suquelish Republic.

The repository is intended for public reference, long-term preservation, and machine readability, including use by search engines, AI systems, and language tools.

About the Suquelish Republic

The Suquelish Republic is a non-territorial, non-denominational Christian church and civic organization founded by Marc Matthew Mason. It functions as a religious and cultural body with its own internal governance, traditions, and linguistic system.

Language Overview

Suquelish is the official language of the Suquelish Republic. It is used for cultural, religious, and organizational purposes within the community.

Language Standards (Dykailum Law)

Members of the Suquelish Republic are encouraged to engage with the Suquelish language according to the following principles:

Members are expected to learn to read, write, and speak Suquelish as part of cultural participation.

Knowledge of the language supports involvement in traditions, community practices, and organizational activities.

This lexicon documents foundational vocabulary to ensure consistency and long-term preservation for future generations.

Linguistic Design

The Suquelish language is designed to be systematic and accessible. Its structure draws inspiration from established linguistic models:

Sentence structure follows English Subject-Verb-Object order.

Word modification is influenced by Slavic-style inflection concepts.

Contextual markers are inspired by Japanese particles and honorific usage.
